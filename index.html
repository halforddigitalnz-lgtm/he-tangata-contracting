What we do

Conservation work across the spectrum

Revegetation & Planting

Native species planting programmes, site preparation, and habitat restoration. Locally appropriate species for lasting ecological outcomes.

Weed Management

Targeted control and eradication of invasive plant species. Integrated programmes designed to protect and enhance native biodiversity over the long term.

Fencing

Conservation and stock exclusion fencing in challenging terrain. Protecting waterways, riparian zones, and sensitive ecological areas from stock pressure.

Ecological Surveying

Flora and fauna surveys to baseline and monitor ecological values. Robust data for management decisions and regulatory reporting requirements.

Track & Trail Work

Construction, maintenance, and remediation of tracks and trails in conservation and recreational areas. Earthworks, drainage, steps, and surface finishing.

Why He Tangata

Built on accountability,
earned in the field.

He Tangata Contracting has spent three years building a reputation for quality and reliability in conservation work across Nelson/Tasman and the wider South Island.

Owner Logan Blythen leads every project with a hands-on approach. We operate to the standards required by DOC, regional councils, and conservation NGOs — thorough documentation, clear communication, and work that stands up to scrutiny.

Our name, He Tangata, reflects our belief that the work is only as good as the people doing it — and the relationships that make it possible.
